Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Name, email address, and login credentials
  • Location data (including background location during active attendance sessions)
  • Camera input for face detection used for attendance verification

2. How We Use Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage user accounts
  • To provide attendance marking functionality
  • To verify user identity using facial recognition
  • To track attendance and ensure worksite compliance using location services

3. Location Data

Our app collects location data only during an active attendance session initiated by the user after marking attendance.

  • Location is captured at the time of attendance check-in using face verification
  • During an active work session, the app performs periodic location checks approximately every 30 minutes
  • These checks are used to verify that the user remains within an authorized 200-meter work radius assigned by the organization
  • Background location tracking is automatically disabled when the user checks out or when the attendance session ends

Location data is not continuously tracked and is not used outside active attendance sessions. It is not used for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes and is not shared with third parties.

4. Face Detection

The app uses facial recognition technology for employee attendance verification.

  • Facial data is processed to generate secure biometric templates for identity verification
  • Raw images captured by the camera are used only for real-time verification and are not used for any unrelated purpose
  • Biometric templates are encrypted and stored securely on backend servers
  • Face data is not shared with any third parties
  • Face data is retained only while the user remains active within the organization and is deleted upon account deactivation or termination

Face recognition is used solely for attendance marking and identity verification and is not used for advertising, profiling, or any unrelated purposes.

5. Data Storage

We store only necessary user data required for app functionality. Sensitive data is protected using appropriate security measures including encryption in transit and at rest.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent user data. We may share limited data only within the organization based on role-based access control or when required by law.

7. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

8. Account Management

Accounts are created and managed by organization administrators. Users do not register independently and do not make payments within the application.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
